Pricing & Revenue Strategy

Standard Plan

  • Management fee: 25% of gross booking revenue (typical CR range 20–30%).
  • Cleaning fees: 100% retained by LXV (charged to guests).
  • Included: Guest support, booking management, dynamic pricing, basic maintenance coordination, bill pay, monthly reports.
  • Extras billed to owner: Major repairs/renovations, utilities (unless agreed), consumables, linen program, HOA fees.
  • Upsell revenue: Transfers, tours, chef services — markup retained by LXV.
  • CPA support: Optional US CPA consultation add‑on for US tax needs.
